    About Me


    My name is Mary Machado and I graduated from Rutgers University. I obtained my bachelor's degree in Computer Science in May 2020.

    During the past two years, I have been lucky to participate in two different internships were I was able to work in teams and learned about the importance of communication between coworkers to accomplish planned objectives. Also, I learned that I am able to turn out well in a fast pace environment.

    In my first internship I worked in a team researching about the adsorption properties of a material called "Biochar" which basically is charcoal made in a closed environment. Our experiment was to see how much of a contaminant would be adsorb by the biochar. In order to see the changes overtime, we used a special machine to make readings every hour during 3-4 days. This created big amounts of data, which we could analyzed using scripts in Python and created user friendly tables and graphs to show all the changes.

    In my second internship, I was responsible to create a server in the cloud using Amazon Web Services. After that, I was in charged of the installation, configuration and deployment of ArcGIS Enterprise, ArcGIS Server and ArcGIS GeoEvent Server.

    I would like to start applying my technology knowledge and my problem solving skills I have acquired during my two internships and during the development of school projects.
